Our Story

SkillNest was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to democratize education and make world-class learning resources available to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

We started with just a handful of courses in web development and have since expanded to cover technology, business, design, and more. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of learners from around the world.

Our platform is built on open-source principles and powered by passionate educators who believe in the transformative power of education. Every course is a labor of love, created to help you achieve your personal and professional goals.
